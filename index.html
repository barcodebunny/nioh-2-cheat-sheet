Miscellaneous Achievements

  • The Beginning of a Samurai: Reach level 10
  • Full-Fledged Samurai: Reach level 100
  • A Weapon's Mind: Achieve full sentience with any yokai weapon
  • Burst Breaker: Successfully use the Brute, Feral, and Phantom burst counters 5 times each.
  • Match Made in Heaven: Soul Match a piece of equipment with the blacksmith.
  • Latest Masterpiece: Forge a piece of equipment with the blacksmith.
  • Remodeling Novice: Remodel a piece of equipment with the blacksmith.
  • Tea Connoisseur: Appraise 50 tea utensils.
  • Lover of Letters: Display a calligraphy scroll in your hut.
  • Trinket Triumph: Purchase something from the Kodama Bazaar.
  • Teamwork: Complete 10 missions with benevolent spirits, through expeditions, or with summoned players.
  • Help Wanted: Summon from 10 benevolent graves.
  • Spa Healer: Bathe in a hot spring.
  • Yokai Quelling Master: Defeat every unique type of yokai.
  • Sudama Swapper: Drop an item for a Sudama.
  • Core Score: Obtain a soul core for the first time.
  • Friend to the Kodama: Obtain a Kodama soul core from a Sudama swap.
  • Fuse It or Lose It: Soul Fuse a soul core to level 9.
  • Let There Be Light: Dispel every Dark Realm in main story missions.
  • Grazer Eraser: Kill Mezuki and Gozuki in the same mission, in one of three ways - Optional Gozuki in The Village of Cursed Blossoms, complete The Refined Man of the Underworld, or fight them both in The Golden Castle.
  • Dungball Roller: Feed Ryunojo 30 dung balls in Bird in a Cage. (Noble dung balls count for 2 regular dung balls)
  • Seasoned Traveler: Discover 10 hidden items by interacting with unmarked locations on the world map.

Can I use this for multiple characters?

Yes, you can change your character profile from the options tab.

Is checklist status saved?

Yes, the checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Clearing browser cache may remove the saved data, and you'll need to check everything all over again x_x. You can alleviate this risk by exporting the checklist at any time, from the options tab.
